"Time to go home" NFT and 2022 Raffle Ticket NFTs

Will Of Myanmar collaborated with the Bo Phyu Lay, from People Defense Force (PDF).

He drew a painting named, “Time to go home” with the materials that he can have in his PDF’s camp while he is fighting for the revolution.

Will of Myanmar created his painting as unique NFT and 2022 lottery tickets were issued and listed as Polygon NFTs with WrappedEthereum 0.002 (which is approximately about USD 5.00) on the Opensea platform.
