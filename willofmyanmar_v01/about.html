About Us

Own the history

 

In order to support the current resistance movement in Myanmar, we started “Will of Myanmar” NFT project. The aim of the project is not only to support the resistance, but also to bring prosperity to the participants.

Will of Myanmar is the world's first NFTs which was born to help restore peace and democracy in Myanmar. “Will of Myanmar” is made to contribute.We pledge to support without any cuts for the team.

Here we summarize our goals.

  • Raise funding for the resistance through the sales of NFTs in the open market auctions.
  • Support artists who had been greatly impacted by COVID pandemic and the coup.
  • Educate our citizens/holders on futuristic technology such as NFTs and share the benefits of said technology.

You can contribute to this democratic movement and be part of the historic moment simply by owning one of the precious Will of Myanmar’s NFTs.

Be part of the millions who are fighting for justice.
