What's Origin of Will of Myanmar?

We came together to form a project team that would offer funding to support our people, ensuring that military rule and tyranny would be overthrown.
The team titled it “Will Of Myanmar” with the purpose of portraying actual will of the people of Myanmar and ensuring that it is carried out.

Why did we choose NFTs as a medium ?

Non Fungible Tokens, or NFTs, are one of the most innovative use of blockchain technology.
NFTs are also one of the most innovative economic concepts.
The advent of massive markets for non-fungible tokens (NFTs), which are one-of-a-kind blockchain-based digital assets, represents a watershed event in how we think about digital transactions and value stores.
The market for NFTs has risen in the last two years, and while it’s been dubbed a bubble, it doesn’t appear to be going down anytime soon. The surge in interest from venture capitalists (VCs) is unlikely to abate anytime soon.

How do we trust WOM?

1. WillofMyanmar Team don't accept direct donations either with crypto payments or fiats.
2. Our sources of funding is solely via NFTs sales.
3. Due to the Transparency of Public Blockchains , anyone, everyone from any part of the world can witness the relative blockchain explorers to check quantity of NFTs sales and correspondent sales funds at anytime.
4. As we are verified by CRPH, we will report all of our donation statuses to it and once we get approvals from both CRPH and the recepients, we'll publicly announce all of our transaction statuses. Extent of details of the recepients will be stated as per safety measures.

How we can purchase Will of Myanmar NFT?

Everyone can directly purchase current Will of Myanmar Ticket NFts via open secondary marketplace,opensea and/or can inquire at official facebool page messenger and telegram chat room for buying with local fiat currencies. Our Admin Team is ready to help almost 24/7.
